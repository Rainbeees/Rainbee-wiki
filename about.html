About Me

Hi there, I'm Rainbees. I am a Toronto girl with two cats.
You may have gotten here through one of the many projects that I work on. Currently I stream on Twitch from Monday to Friday. I release videos on Youtube.

I am an artist that primarily works with traditional medias like ink, watercolor and pencil as well as the occasional digital art. You can find my projects here.

I consider learning to be the most important things in life. You will find me trying to learn something new all the time. I am currently teaching myself how to do front-end development with the eventual goal of branching into other languages. Aligning with this journey and my passion for art, I am also challenging myself to learn digital art and UI design.
