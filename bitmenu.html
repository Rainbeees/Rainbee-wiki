FAQ

Why are there so little options?

I want to test this out before I use it too much. I dont want someone to feel like they spent their real money on something that didnt provide an adequate value to them.

Can I get a refund if I don't like the outcome?

No. You accept all outcomes that may not be to your liking due to game difficulty, player skill, etc. If there is a glitch where it doesnt spawn, I will dev mode it in. If the game crashes and we must roll back the save a large amount to before your event, no refund/dev spawn will be provided.

Any plans to add more?

Yes! Plans to add youtube(if I get multistream working) donations and kofi donations to trigger events are incoming. More events are incoming pending testing.
