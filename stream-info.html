Current Games

I am currently mostly streaming Rimworld with Twitch Toolkit and Rimconnect. That means the YOU the viewer can have an impact on what happens in my game. You can make your own colonist in my settlement - dress it, change it's mood, chat with other colonists and more. You could even send a tornado my way to tear it all down.
Each new Rimworld colony has a theme and different rules to keep it interesting for myself and the viewers.

  • Sending in items is locked if we haven't researched it yet
  • Rimworld of Magic and Vanilla Expanded Psycasts are unlimited
  • Strive to Survive Difficulty
  • No cooldowns on sending items or events
  • Spawn in with $pawn and $use any of the xenogerms to change your xenotype(race)
  • Our Ideoligin is focused on building a magnificent city and staying in it.
